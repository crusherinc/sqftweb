Our Story

Quality Work, Honest Pricing

SQFT Contracting is a GTA-based interior finishing company built on the belief that every homeowner deserves honest, high-quality work at a fair price. We specialize in drywall installation, interior painting, tile work, flooring, and demolition services for residential homes across Toronto and the surrounding areas.

Our team brings precision workmanship to every project we take on. From hanging the first sheet of drywall to applying the final coat of paint, we pay attention to the details that other contractors overlook. We measure twice, cut once, and never cut corners on materials or technique.

We also take pride in maintaining clean, organized job sites throughout every project. Your home is your space, and we treat it with the respect it deserves. When the work is done, we leave the site spotless so you can enjoy your newly finished rooms right away.
