Our Story

Building Trust, One Project at a Time

SQFT Contracting was founded with a simple mission: to provide homeowners with professional painting and drywall services they can trust. We understand that your home is your most important investment, and we treat every project with the care and attention it deserves.

With over 15 years of experience in the Greater Toronto Area, we've built our reputation on flawless finishes, transparent communication, and respect for our clients' homes and timelines.

What sets us apart is our commitment to doing things right. From the initial consultation to the final walkthrough, you'll work with a dedicated team that's invested in your satisfaction.
