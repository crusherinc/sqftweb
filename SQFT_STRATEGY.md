## ЭТАП 3. СТРУКТУРА САЙТА (SILO)

```
SQFT Contracting
│
├── Home (/)
│   └── Overview всех сервисов, trust, CTA
│
├── Services (/services)
│   ├── Kitchen Remodeling (/services/kitchen-remodeling)
│   ├── Bathroom Remodeling (/services/bathroom-remodeling)
│   ├── Basement Renovation (/services/basement-renovation)
│   ├── Home Additions (/services/home-additions)
│   ├── Interior Painting (/services/interior-painting)
│   └── Drywall & Repairs (/services/drywall-repair)
│
├── Projects (/projects)
│   └── Gallery with filters
│
├── About (/about)
│   └── Story, team, credentials
│
└── Contact (/contact)
    └── Form, phone, address, map
```

### Почему такая структура:

1. **Silo architecture** — каждый сервис на отдельной странице для SEO
2. **Kitchen/Bathroom первые** — highest search volume
3. **Projects отдельно** — social proof, internal linking
4. **Flat hierarchy** — max 2 clicks to any page
